About Website

IEEE IAS VIT, a prominent professional student chapter, fosters a bridge between passion and excellence. Our enthusiastic team organizes several research conferences, expert talks, and seminars, connecting research to practical applications. We have spearheaded VIT's major international events, earning the title of Best Chapter for the 2022-23 academic year.

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a dedicated organization uplifting women engineers and scientists. We provide a support system, inspire academic pursuits, and foster a vibrant community of women innovators. IEEE WIE VIT aims to bring out the best in women by enhancing technical skills and encouraging them to strive for excellence.
